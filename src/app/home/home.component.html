What is NewVote?

NewVote is a democracy app, and it’s all about you.

Your communities, your issues, your ideas and your decisions.

Our mission is to rebuild the relationship between everyday people and their leaders, so that we all have confidence in our democracies and the decisions they make.

We’re doing this across all levels of government worldwide, as well as “private” democratic communities, like unions, businesses, community groups, political parties, student councils, clubs and societies.

Connect with your community in a way that you can truly be heard - easily and directly - whilst leaders get the data they need.

Let’s enhance democracy together.

Your voice matters, and all you need is a profile to start voting!

How do I Vote?

You’ll need to make an account using an email address and your mobile number to get started. This is to make sure every user only votes once (in the name of democracy)!

How it Works

The voting process is quite simple. Explore this app to view issues, solutions and proposals!

ISSUES

Issues can be any problem or topic in your community that you think needs to be addressed.

SOLUTIONS

Solutions are the decisions that you think your community should make.

PROPOSALS

Proposals provide further clarity to solutions and specific actions.
