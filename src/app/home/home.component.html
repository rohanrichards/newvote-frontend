What is NewVote?

icon

NewVote is a platform for digital democracy, and it is all about you.

Your beliefs, your ideas, your solutions and your decisions.

We're concerned about the large and growing disconnect between everyday Australians, our politicians and government decision making. Confidence in our political system is at an all time low, and we think the problem is that there is a barrier that blocks everyday Australians from engaging easily and directly with their representatives.

We are on a mission to rebuild that relationship by creating a place where people can truly be heard.

Every voice matters, and all you need is a profile to start voting!
