About NewVote

NewVote is a democracy app, and it’s all about you.

Your communities, your issues, your ideas and your decisions. Our mission is to rebuild the relationship between everyday people and their leaders, so that we all have confidence in our democracies and the decisions they make.

We’re doing this across all levels of government worldwide, as well as “private” democratic communities, like unions, businesses, community groups, political parties, student councils, clubs and societies.

Connect with your community in a way that you can truly be heard - easily and directly - whilst leaders get the data they need.

Let’s enhance democracy together. Your voice matters, and all you need is a profile to start voting!
